Bio

Outside of CS/Math, I like learning about other cultures. I study Japanese in my spare time, and have achieved an N5 proficiency on the Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT). I am working towards an N4 proficiency now. I'm also known for my bank shot in foosball .
